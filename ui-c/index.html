How it work

The GetJob simple project posting process allows you to control all aspects of your listing. Start by selecting if you would like to hire one or many Freelancers to work on your project. .

Post an Instant Hire Project

instant Hire projects are great for large scale tasks which do not require much review. Freelancers can start on your project immediately and submit their work for you to review.

Choose the Perfect Freelancer

Review your bids and check out the Freelancer’s profile to select the perfect Freelancer for your project .

Post a Bid Project

The GetJob provides the ability to post a project where Freelancers can submit Bids and you can choose the bid or bids which fit your needs.

Evaluate Freelancer Work

The Latium Contract Management System provides you with an easy-to-use method to review your Freelancer’s work by checking submitted files, Screenshot Proofs, or any other method you require.
